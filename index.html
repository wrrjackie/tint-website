About Us

At Precision Solar Tint, a veteran-owned and operated business in South Texas, our mission is to deliver exceptional tinting solutions while honoring our commitment to service, integrity, and excellence. We are dedicated to providing our valued customers with the highest quality window tinting services, ensuring their satisfaction and peace of mind. With our deep-rooted military values, we prioritize attention to detail, precision, and professionalism in every project we undertake. Our goal is to enhance your driving experience by offering superior UV protection, temperature control, glare reduction, and interior preservation, all while maintaining the privacy and security you deserve. Trust Precision Solar Tint to transform your vehicle and experience tinting services that reflect the values and expertise of our veteran-owned and operated team.

We are not going to be the cheapest in town, for that we exclusively use high end products and take pride in our craftsmanship.
